Welcome to my Elden Ring first look, Don Mykal!

I’ve been playing about 20 hours and that’s what I can say: First corridor location looks like typical for souls game and feels home

But everything changes when you going out into the open world. It becomes clear that the journey will be epic. You can see that the world is alive and you want to explore it. Every location is deep it spends hours to investigate one. Enemies are various everyone needs their own approach. Bosses makes me cry. It’s fucking hard.
I’m really into new battle mechanics. Fights on the mount is amazing, spirit summoning ability makes gameplay more variable and strategies more refined. I also like the change of day and night it adds dynamic to the world. I enjoy the game. Thank you very much for giving me opportunity to play this amazing game!
